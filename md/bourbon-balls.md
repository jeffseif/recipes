# Bourbon Balls

Yields 6 dozen

From Grandma Corbin, January 2026.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups vanilla wafer crumbs
- 2 cups chopped pecans
- 2 cups sifted powdered sugar
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
- 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons bourbon
- Sifted powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Combine and mix wafers, nuts, sugar, and cocoa
1. Add and mix corn syrup and bourbon
1. Form 1 inch balls and roll into powdered sugar
1. Store in airtight container
